About WCCE

Established in 2018 in Indonesia, the World Conference on Creative Economy is a leading global platform where creatives, entrepreneurs, and policy-makers convene to foster dialogue, address challenges, and unveil opportunities. Collectively, they contribute to defining and shaping the future of the global creative economy.

The conference aims to foster international cooperation, highlight the role of creativity in driving an inclusive, sustainable economy, and shift focus from resource-reliant to knowledge-based economies.

WCCE is a biennial event spanning three days, with each alternate conference hosted in a different country.
