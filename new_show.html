Uzbekistan Art and Culture Development Foundation announces an internship programme at the Venice Contemporary Art Biennale 2024

18 December 2023

City Sustainnaibility

Reading time: 2 min.

The programme is aimed at students and graduates of Uzbek universities interested in the field of art and culture. The participants of the internship will have the opportunity to work as part of the team of the Uzbekistan National Pavilion and contribute to the presentation of Uzbek culture in the international arena. ➔ The main tasks of trainee mediators: — Interaction with visitors — Maintenance of the pavilion and the exhibits — Cultural mediation Internship period: April 15 - June 29 / June 30 - September 12 / September 13 - November 24, 2024. ➔ Working conditions: — Paid internship — Covered costs of flights and accommodation in Venice — Free access to other pavilions at the Arsenale – La Biennale di Venezia ➔ Requirements for candidates: — Knowledge of English — Understanding Uzbek culture — Readiness to work in an international environment ➔ To submit an application: Send a motivation letter and resume in English by January 10, 2024 by e-mail education@acdf.uz
